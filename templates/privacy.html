Privacy Statement for CrewOptic

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

At CrewOptic, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy statement explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our application.

1. Who We Are

CrewOptic is an application designed to help rowing clubs manage athlete performance data. The application is operated in the United Kingdom and complies with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following personal data:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Performance and training data (e.g., times, metrics, notes)
  • Any other information you choose to submit or that is generated during training sessions

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used solely for the purposes of:

  • Monitoring and analysing your rowing performance
  • Enabling your coaches to provide feedback and training guidance
  • Maintaining accurate records of training and progress

Your data will never be shared outside your rowing club or with third parties, unless required by law.

4. Consent and Control

You will be asked to grant explicit permission for:

  • Your data to be stored and processed in CrewOptic
  • Your coaches to access your performance data

You can withdraw your consent at any time by contacting your club administrator or CrewOptic support.

5. Data Sharing

Your data is only accessible to:

  • You, the individual athlete
  • Your designated coaches within the club

No data is shared with external parties, sponsors, or commercial entities.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data storage
  • Access controls to limit who can view or edit your data

7. Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your data

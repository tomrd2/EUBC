CrewOptic

Crew Optic is an application for gathering, monitoring and interpreting rowing data in a performance squad. It includes:

  • All land training data for squad members
  • All crew lineups
  • All on-the-water pieces
  • All in-boat telemetry

Key Benefits

Modern rowing coaches have access to large quantities of performance data. However, this data may reside in a number of different places, such as spreadsheets, timing apps or telemetry apps. Crew Optic gathers this data into a single place and so that higher level tools can be used to interpret athlete and crew performance.

Presenting data in one place

Athlete, crew and squad dashboards allow coaches and athletes to see and compare training data. This can include simple training volumes (attendance and time/distance) or higher level modelling of training.

Performance Models

On-the-water data can be modelled to provide insight into individual performance, even when delivered in crew boats. This is gamified to provide athletes with feedback on training performance.

ML and AI

Machine learning and AI models are used to bring together separate data, such as land-training, OTW results and onboard telemetry. This can provide coaches with valuable information about the significance of individual metrics as well as the performance of the athletes themselves.
